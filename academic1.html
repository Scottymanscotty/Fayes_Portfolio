Leeds Arena

Please Note: Unauthorized use and/or duplication of this material without express and written permission from this materials author and/or owner is strictly prohibited.

My Role:

This is a project from my MA Urban Design Degree. My aim was to extend the performance which happened inside the arena to the areas surrounding it. My theme was Earth, Air, Water and Fire. Where each element would offer the public a unique experience. For example, I had sculptures which would move and make noises instrumented by the wind. I created pedestrian only streets and proposed new arcades, something which Leeds is commonly known for. I also created a new City Centre Lake with pedestrian and wildlife links crossing through.
