ABOUT ME

I'm an Urban Designer based in Leeds - Yorkshire , with a passion to enhance the built environment. My aim is to improve peoples quality of life by improving the places they work and play in. I consider myself a triple threat. By this, I mean Architecture, Planning and Design . I have a Masters in Urban Design and Town and Regional Planning and I started my journey by studying Interior Architecture and Design. As if that isn't enough I have been working as a Landscape Architect for the last three years.
