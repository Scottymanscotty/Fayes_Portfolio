A little about me

Hello!

I'm an Urban Designer from Yorkshire, with a passion to enhance and repair the built environment. My aim is to improve peoples quality of life by improving places they work and play in. I consider myself a triple threat. By this, I mean Architecture, Planning and Design. I have a Masters in Urban Design and Town and Regional Planning and I started my journey by studying a degree in Interior Architecture. As if that isn't enough I have been working as a Landscape Architect for the last three years. I also enjoy taking photos of landscapes however I am usualy drawn to plants and insects.
