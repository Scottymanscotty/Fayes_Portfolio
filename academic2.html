Birmingham Cities Alive

My Role:

Birmingham Cities Alive was a live competition which me and two other students entered. The brief was to redesign the existing public space outside of Birmingham Library. Cities Alive is a concept created by Arub. Our main concept was to produce a Biophilic square. We envisioned that the space would have it's own ecosystem and biodiversity, with the introduction of green spaces and natural green walkways which would eventually spread throughout the whole city. The current space had been designed by an Artist so we decided re-use the design but in a fragmented way to allow the natural growth of grass between the paving.

The second part of this project was to have an individual piece, therefore I concentrated on ways to encourage and reintroduce wildlife into the city centre.
