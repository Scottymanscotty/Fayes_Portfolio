Sustainable Vision for Todmorden

My Role:

I worked with two other students in my MA Town & Regional Planning degree to produce a group report. We covered all bases including Housing, Public Space, Tourism and Transport. We tried to enhance Todmordens unique style by including such things as giant vegetable sculptures to further elaborate on its success as the founding town of Incredible Edible.
