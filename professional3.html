Tree Survey and Root Protection Areas

My Role:

By using the computer software Keytree, I can correctly mark on the canopies and root protection areas of existing trees and hedges. These are coloured by their category which is determined by an Arboroculturist. The drawing is key for Developers and Architects to plan where their proposal will be without the damage or loss of any existing vegetation. This drawing is connect to a Tree Survey report.
